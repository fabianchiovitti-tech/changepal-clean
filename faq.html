Frequently Asked Questions

Is ChangePal a government service?

No. ChangePal is a private service that simplifies your address updates by notifying organizations on your behalf.

Is it legal for you to update my address?

Yes. In Quebec, you can authorize someone to act on your behalf with a signed mandate form. We only use this for address updates.

How do I know my data is safe?

We’re committed to protecting your personal information. Uploads will be encrypted, and your data is never sold or shared.

What organizations do you notify?

We handle updates for government agencies, telecom companies, banks, insurance providers, and more. You choose who gets notified.

Can I cancel after I submit?

Yes. You can cancel any time before we begin submissions. Once submitted, we can't undo changes made with third parties.
