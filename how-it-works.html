How It Works

ChangePal is your personal address change assistant — making sure everyone important gets your new address without the hassle.

Step 1: Tell Us Your New Address

Enter your old and new address just once. No repeating the same form over and over again.

Step 2: Choose Who to Notify

We’ll guide you through a quick checklist of common contacts:

  • Government services
  • Banks & insurance
  • Phone & internet
  • Subscriptions & memberships
  • Friends & family

You can also add custom contacts.

Step 3: We Send the Notifications

We’ll handle the hard part:

  • Auto-fill and send forms or emails
  • Generate official letters when needed
  • Track what was sent and when

You’ll get a confirmation for each one.

Step 4: You’re Done

Relax — your move is now complete. We’ll make sure your information gets where it needs to go.
