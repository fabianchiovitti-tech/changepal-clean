How ChangePal Works

1. Fill Out the Start Form

Tell us who you are, your old address, and your new one. It only takes a minute, and helps us tailor your update process.

2. Sign the Mandate Form

This simple legal form gives ChangePal permission to act on your behalf — just for your address change. It’s legally valid in Quebec.

3. We Notify Everyone for You

Once your info and documents are in, we begin contacting government agencies, banks, telecom providers, and more — saving you hours of work.
